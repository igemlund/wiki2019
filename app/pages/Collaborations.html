Collaborations

Collaborating with other teams is an integral part of iGEM. On this page we list the teams we've collaborated with over the course of the year.

iGEM team Stockholm

We were invited by the iGEM team of Stockholm to participated with them in the summer science camp event. Both teams introduced synthetic biology to teenage students through short seminars and experiments, and presented their projects to them. They also participated in an episode of the podcast The iGEM Lund Show.

Erik Hartman, from iGEM Lund, spoke at an event they organized about the laws regulating synthetic biology.

They’re also an integral part of SynthEthics.
